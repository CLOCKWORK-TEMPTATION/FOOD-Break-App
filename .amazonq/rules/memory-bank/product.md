# BreakApp Product Overview

## Project Purpose
BreakApp is a comprehensive food ordering and delivery management system specifically designed for production teams in the entertainment industry. It streamlines meal coordination, manages food costs, and optimizes the dining experience for film crews, TV productions, and other creative teams.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Eliminates meal coordination chaos** during production shoots
- **Reduces food costs** through intelligent ordering and bulk negotiations
- **Ensures dietary compliance** and health tracking for crew members
- **Provides real-time delivery tracking** with GPS integration
- **Manages exceptions and special orders** with role-based permissions

## Key Features & Capabilities

### Core Menu System
- **Fixed Rotating Menu**: Curated restaurant partnerships with quality tracking
- **Geographic Menu**: Location-based restaurant discovery within 2-3km radius
- **Daily/Weekly Rotation**: Fresh options to prevent meal fatigue

### Exception Management System
- **Role-Based Access**: Regular crew vs VIP personnel ("النجوم")
- **Exception Types**: Full exceptions, limited exceptions, self-paid exceptions
- **Quota Management**: Controlled exception frequency (once every 3 weeks for regular users)
- **Cost Tracking**: Automatic differential payment calculations

### Smart Order Workflow
- **QR Code Access**: Project-specific access control
- **Time-Window Ordering**: First hour of shooting day submission
- **Automated Notifications**: Half-hourly reminders for non-submitters
- **GPS Delivery Tracking**: Real-time location and ETA updates

### Intelligence Features (Planned)
- **AI Recommendations**: Weather-based and personalized meal suggestions
- **Predictive Ordering**: Auto-suggestions based on user patterns
- **Smart Restaurant Discovery**: Multi-platform rating aggregation

### Health & Wellness
- **Nutrition Dashboard**: Calorie and macronutrient tracking
- **Diet Filters**: Halal, vegan, gluten-free, keto, allergy management
- **Fitness Integration**: Apple Health, Google Fit, Fitbit connectivity

### Social & Engagement
- **Points & Rewards**: Gamified ordering with redeemable benefits
- **Collaborative Reviews**: Team-based restaurant rating system
- **Group Order Deals**: Bulk discount coordination

## Target Users

### Primary Users
- **Production Crew Members**: Daily meal ordering and tracking
- **VIP Personnel**: Enhanced exception privileges and premium options
- **Production Managers**: Budget oversight and team coordination

### Secondary Users
- **Restaurant Partners**: Order management and quality feedback
- **Logistics Coordinators**: Delivery scheduling and route optimization
- **Producers**: Financial reporting and cost analysis

## Use Cases

### Daily Operations
- Crew members scan QR code to access project-specific menu
- Submit meal orders during designated time window
- Receive notifications about order status and delivery ETA
- Track nutritional intake and dietary compliance

### Exception Handling
- VIP users request unlimited exceptions with cost alerts
- Regular users utilize quarterly exception quota
- Automatic cost differential calculations for budget tracking
- Weekly financial settlements with payroll integration

### Production Management
- Real-time budget monitoring with threshold alerts
- Delivery coordination with shooting schedule integration
- Waste analysis and cost optimization recommendations
- Multi-project financial reporting and forecasting

## Technology Foundation
- **Mobile**: React Native with TypeScript
- **Backend**: Node.js with Express.js
- **Database**: PostgreSQL with Prisma ORM
- **Authentication**: JWT-based security
- **Cloud**: AWS/GCP/Azure infrastructure
- **Maps**: Google Maps API integration
- **Payments**: Stripe/PayPal integration
